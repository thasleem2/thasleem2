how to prepare bread omlet

hai!This is A.Thasleem.Welcome to Thasleem's Recipe.Today am going to tell about how to prepare bread omlet.lets start guys.


Ingredients

  1. pan
  2. oil
  3. one egg
  4. coriander leaves few
  5. green chutney as needed
  6. two piece of bread
  7. red chili powder
  8. butter
  9. salt to taste
  10. cheese

prepartion

  • Crack open a egg in a bowl,add salt,chili powder&coriander leaves into the egg and whisk it till it become frothy.
  • heat the pan,add the butter ,let it melt.
  • pour the egg mixture into the pan,keep the flame in low.
  • take a bread slice,dip one side of the bread slice in the egg mixture on the pan slightly,flip the breasd to other side and place it on the pan.
  • that way,both sides of the bread have egg coating.
  • Similarly place second slice of bread on other side of the pan,let it cook for a munute and then gently flip the egg.
  • Let cook the other side of tyhe bread for a minute and then flip again.
  • Spread the green chutney on one slice of bread and place the cheese on other slice of bread.
  • Flod the bread sides of the egg omlet towards the center of bread and fold again into half
  • Remove from the place and slice into four or as you wish
